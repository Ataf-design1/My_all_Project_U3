Chemistry Assignment

Chemistry is the branch of science that deal with the properties composition and behaviour of matter it is a fascinating field that explores the building blocks of the universe

Chemical elements

The periodic table is a tabular arrangement of chemical elements. Each element is represented by its chemical symbol, such as Fe for iron, H for hydrogen, and O for oxygen. Elements are classified into groups and periods based on their properties.

Chemical Equations

Chemical reaction are represented using chemical equation here's an example of a chemical reaction.

2H2 + O2 = 2H2O

This equation represent the reaction between hydrogen gas (H2) and oxygen gas (O2) to form water (H2O)

Important concepts

Sociochemistry is the calculation of reaction and product in chemical reactions. It involves balancing chemical equation to ensure mass and charge are conserved. Subscript indicate the number of atoms of each element in a molecule, while superscript represent charge on the number of atom in an ion.

Conclusion
